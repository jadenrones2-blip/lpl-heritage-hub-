LPL Financial - Brand Guidelines

COLOR SCHEME:
- Primary Blue: #003366 (LPL Corporate Blue)
- Secondary Blue: #0066CC (LPL Accent Blue)
- Accent: #00A3E0 (LPL Light Blue)
- Text: #333333 (Dark Gray)
- Background: #FFFFFF (White) with #F5F5F5 (Light Gray) for sections
- Success/Positive: #28A745 (Green)
- Warning: #FFC107 (Yellow/Amber)
- Error: #DC3545 (Red)

TYPOGRAPHY:
- Primary Font: Arial, Helvetica, or similar clean sans-serif
- Professional, corporate appearance
- Headings: Bold, 16-24px
- Body: Regular, 14-16px
- Clean, readable, trustworthy

BUTTON STYLES:
- Primary: LPL Corporate Blue (#003366) background, white text
- Secondary: White background, LPL Corporate Blue border
- Rounded corners (4-6px)
- Professional, not overly stylized
- Clear call-to-action text

UI ELEMENTS:
- Clean, professional dashboard layout
- Card-based design for information display
- Subtle shadows and borders
- Professional data tables
- Clear hierarchy and organization
- Suitable for financial advisor's laptop/desktop
