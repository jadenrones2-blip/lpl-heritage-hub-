LPL Financial - Account Opening Requirements & NIGO Rules

REQUIRED FIELDS FOR ACCOUNT OPENING FORMS:
1. Full Legal Name (must match government-issued ID)
2. Social Security Number (SSN) or Tax ID
3. Physical Address (P.O. Box not acceptable for primary address)
4. Date of Birth
5. Beneficiary Name and Relationship
6. Signature (wet signature required, not electronic for certain forms)
7. Account Type Selection
8. Investment Objective
9. Risk Tolerance Acknowledgment
10. Suitability Information

COMMON NIGO ERRORS (Not In Good Order):
- Missing or incomplete signature
- Signature date missing or more than 90 days old
- Beneficiary information incomplete (name but no relationship, or vice versa)
- Physical address missing or invalid (P.O. Box only)
- SSN format incorrect or missing
- Date of birth missing or invalid format
- Account type not selected
- Investment objective not specified
- Risk tolerance not acknowledged
- Typos in legal name that don't match ID
- Missing pages from multi-page documents

PRIORITY LEVELS:
- HIGH: Missing signature, missing SSN, missing beneficiary
- MEDIUM: Missing address, missing date of birth, incomplete beneficiary info
- LOW: Typos, formatting issues, missing optional fields
